OUR HAWAII HONEYMOON 2014

Day 1, Friday

Today, we will take our time arriving at the Kona airport, picking up our car at Enterprise, and driving to our first hotel destination: Puna Rainforest Retreat. We found this destination on vrbo.com. It is around a 3 hour drive from the airport to Puna!

If we have extra time, we can explore the nearby town to find out where things are (grocery store, restaurants, etc.)

Day 2, Saturday

  • Volcanoes National Park
    • Kilauea Crater via Crater Rim Drive (an 11-mile road that circles the summit, passes through desert, rainforest and scenic stops)
    • NO gas, food, or water nearby. Be prepared!
  • Visit the city of Volcano and surrounding areas?

Day 3, Sunday

  • Wai O'Pae Tidepools in Kapoho. Here, we can snorkel with the fishes!
  • Kalpana Beach in the late afternoon/evening. There is always a party here Sunday nights! Bring food/water, towels, swimwear!
    • hike, explore, see waterfalls!

Day 4, Monday

  • Waipio Valley
    • ATV TOUR! with Tom Barefoot's Tours
      • Check-in at 9:00. Starts at 9:15am. 3 hours long.
      • Wear pants over swimsuit AND tennis shoes
      • Snacks and drinks provided
    • Hiking. Lots of hiking in Waipio Valley. We'll see waterfalls, streams, forests, wild horses.
      • Bring backpack with water, towels, clothes, food?

Day 5, Tuesday

  • Travel Day
    • Travel from Puna to Kailua-Kona to our next rental destination, also found on vrbo.com.
    • Stop and see the sights along the way!

Day 6, Wednesday

  • FREE DAY! Today, there are NO plans :) We can make plans or just wander around all day.

Day 7, Thursday

  • ISLAND BREEZE LUAU at King Kamehameha's Kona Beach Hotel

    • 75-5660 Palani Road
    Kailua-Kona, HI 96740
    • Check-in is at 4:30pm. Luau ends at 8:30pm.
    • Bring your confirmation sheet signed.
    • Booked this Luau through HawaiiDiscount.com.
    • Parking for luau at the hotel lot is $5. Validation is given at the luau check-in.


Day 8, Friday

  • FREE DAY! No plans.

Day 9, Saturday

  • Free day, then travel to Kona airport in the evening for flight home.
  • Return rental car to Enterprise
  • Flight leaves at 10:00pm
